November 2, 2024 • In-person @ McMaster University

Welcome to HammerHacks where technology is the new steel!

Our mission is to inspire Hamilton's youth to engage in STEM-related activities. We aim to encourage more Hamiltonian students to participate and compete in STEM-focused events and opportunities, both within our community and beyond.

Our vision is to see future Hamiltonian programmers, engineers, scientists, and startup CEOs create groundbreaking technology.

Join us and 150 students for a 12-hour, in-person hackathon where you can create and showcase tech projects, learn from informative workshops, and connect with fellow tech enthusiasts and industry leaders. Participate to win exciting prizes and awards, and engage in fun activities.

Learn, collaborate, and innovate as we add glamour to the Hammer!
