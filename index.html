FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, domain experts, and others collaborate intensively on software projects.

What is Hammerhacks?

HammerHacks is Hamilton's first ever high school focused hackathon! We're bringing together students from all over the city to collaborate and create amazing projects.

When is Hammerhacks?

HammerHacks is coming November 2nd!

How Much Does it Cost?

HammerHacks is completely free!

How can I get Involved?

You can get involved by signing up for our newsletter to stay up to date on all things HammerHacks!

Do I Need a Team?

You can work alone, or with a team of up to 4 people! If you don't already have a team, don't worry, you can connect with other people in our slack or in-person!

Who can Participate?

Anyone over the age of 13 enrolled in a secondary or post-secondary school (university or college) can participate!
