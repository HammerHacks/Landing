Welcome to Hamilton's first high school focused hackathon

Join HammerHacks for a 12-hour, in-person hackathon open to Hamiltonian high school students. As the first event of its kind in Hamilton, we gather to create and present tech projects, learn from innovative workshops, and connect with fellow tech enthusiasts and professional industry leaders. HammerHacks is open to students of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced coders. Join us to win exciting prizes and participate in engaging activities as we learn, collaborate, and innovate, adding glamour to the Hammer!

A hackathon is a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers and others involved in software development, including graphic designers, interface designers, project managers, domain experts, and others collaborate intensively on software projects.

HammerHacks is Hamilton's first ever high school focused hackathon! We're bringing together students from all over the city to collaborate and create amazing projects.
